Need to know when you are a foreign tourist
Passport Requirements
Passports must be valid for a minimum of six months from the date of arrival for entry into Singapore. Please check with your local Singapore government representative for visa requirements.
Declaration of Medical
With the complicated situation of covid-19 in the world, to ensure the safety of you and us,all passengers entering Singapore must make a medical declaration. You can make a medical declaration 4 to 7 days before arrival and send it to us to save your time.
Airport Transport Service
To make your arrival and departure a little less hectic, we can arrange for a private transfer to and from the hotel. Contact the Concierge for details.
Tramsport Hubs and Directions
It takes about 40 minutes to travel from Soekarno -Hatta International Airport (CGK) airport to the hotel by car.
