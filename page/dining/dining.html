MR. PAUL HOOGWAERTS
EXECUTIVE PASTRY CHEF
“The key element makes for an outstanding dessert is texture. Every dessert should offer a balanced contrast: creamy, crunchy, weight of elements.”
A native of Mezzocorona in northeastern Italy, Paul is grateful to his late grandmother for sparking his potential and inspiring him into the world of pastry. Start with culinary memories revolving around how his grandmother made the difference to apple pie and roll and guided him when he was as young as 6 years old a long time ago. After more than ten years worked from a regular chef and becoming a main service chef at several eateries in Europe, he has realized that his greatest passion is pastry.

GOOD QUALITY BREAKFAST
Sit in a high-back armchair covered with green velvet, beneath an eye-catching crystal chandelier or sit on the terrace overlooking the garden and enjoy a typical Southeast Asian or Europe breakfast.
ITALIAN LUNCH
Enjoy lunch in your room or in the rotunda dining room perfected by authentic Italian gourmets led by Executive Chef Mathew Richards and his award-winning culinary team.
AFTERNOON TEA
Sit down for a stylish early afternoon pick-me-up, either on La Patisserie’s air-conditioned patio or in the rotunda feasting room, and appreciate a three-tier choice of sweet and appetizing delights and an worldwide tea menu.
COZY DINNER
In an Italian space with red and beige as the main color of Alto restaurant, sit under the medium light, enjoy dinner, sip a glass of wine and chat with your partner, while looking at the scenery. View of the whole city at the top floor of the building.
SHAKEN AND STIRRED
Make your day even more special with an appointment with your partner at Nautilus Bar. Here we serve all kinds of special drinks and cocktails, handcrafted with the finest ingredients by the talented bartender Alan Cany.
