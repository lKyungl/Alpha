HOTEL LAMPOR
ABOUT US
Discover Lampor hotel with the most preferential and safe services. All 152 rooms at Lampor Hotel are a combination of Asian and European architectural space, bringing elegance, luxury and relaxation with panoramic views of Singapore city.
Located on one of Singapore central arterial roads, set along a tree-lined boulevard within Singapore’s main shopping, business and entertainment district, Lampor Hotel is an ideal destination for both business and leisure travelers. As the one of international standard hotels of Singapore, we put you right in the heart of prime dining, shopping and exploration. Stop in for breakfast beneath a custom Lasvit chandelier, or take in the city at your own pace – starting with views from the wall-to-wall windows in your suite.

HOTEL MANAGER
“With experience and dedicated service, Lampor hotel always gives you the best service. You are always welcome here!”
Born and raised in Portugal, Philip began his career in 2003 as an assistant manager at the Epic Sana Algarve hotel. After holding a number of similar positions at hotels and resorts around the world, he moved to Singapore and joined the Lampor’s big family.

EXECUTIVE PASTRY CHEF

A native of Mezzocorona in northeastern Italy, Paul is grateful to his late grandmother for sparking his potential and inspiring him into the world of pastry. Start with culinary memories revolving around how his grandmother made the difference to apple pie and roll and guided him when he was as young as 6 years old a long time ago. After more than ten years worked from a regular chef and becoming a main service chef at several eateries in Europe, he has realized that his greatest passion is pastry.
